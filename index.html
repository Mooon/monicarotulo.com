About me

Hi, I'm Monica!

I solve complex problems from data. I am an advisor and scientist on high performance machine learning at SURF (Dutch National Supercomputing Center) in Amsterdam. Before stepping into the field of AI for Science, I obtained a Ph.D. in applied math and engineering at the University of Groningen focusing on data-driven control for unknown dynamical systems.

Besides working with data, I'm a landscape photographer, I travel, I love learning new things about science and technology, and occasionally write about my research.

Personal interests:

  • Photography and color theory
  • Astrophysics and astronautics
  • Environmental sustainability
  • UI/UX design
